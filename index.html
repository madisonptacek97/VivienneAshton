TRIP TO
CINQUE TERRA

RED STRIPED DRESS

I went on a trip to the five differnt towns in Cinque Terra in northwest Italy these last two weeks! I stayed for a few days in each town and traveled to Monterosso al Mare, Vernazza, Corniglia, Manarola and Riomaggiore.

Monterosso al Mare was beautiful and the people were really friendly. I was a little woried when I first started my trip because my Italian is limited but it was pretty easy to communicate since a lot of people also knew English.

The food was delicious, and there were little stalls along the street selling fresh ingredients. Everywhere we ate, was very welcoming with a great atmosphere.
